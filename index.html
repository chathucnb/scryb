Features

Built for Real-World
Bookkeeping

From messy documents to posted transactions — Scryb handles the work so your team can focus on what matters.

Self-Learning AI

Learns your bookkeeping rules from historical data and starts coding transactions automatically. The more you use it, the smarter it gets — adapting to your specific chart of accounts and vendor patterns.

Duplicate Detection

Automatically flags potential duplicate transactions before posting — keeping books clean and eliminating double-entry errors before they happen.

Line Item Extraction

Extracts every line item from invoices, bills, and receipts — even when documents are messy, scanned at odd angles, or partially handwritten.

Bank Statements

Turn any bank statement into structured transactions ready for Xero, QuickBooks, or CSV export.

Handwriting Recognition

Reads handwritten invoices and receipts, turning them into structured accounting data.

Cloud Storage

SharePoint, Dropbox, and Google Drive — auto-ingest documents the moment they're uploaded.

Auto-Split

Upload one mixed PDF. Scryb automatically separates invoices, receipts, and bank statements into individual documents — each extracted, coded, and ready to post.
